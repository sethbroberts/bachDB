Each dot represents a piece by Johann Sebastian Bach.

The size of the dot is roughly proportional to the piece's duration.

Dots can be recolored or sorted by the attributes above. You can search for a piece by bwv or title (e.g., 'bwv1', 'toccata and fugue').

Clicking on a dot will load a YouTube performance of the corresponding piece.
